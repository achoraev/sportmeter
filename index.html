SPORT METER is free running app that uses GPS to map and track sport and fitness activities - running, biking, walking, driving, jogging, cycling, riding and etc. Track your workout for maximum speed, average speed, distance and duration with Sport Meter. App to measure speed, distance and time of running, biking, walking, driving, jogging, cycling and riding.

SPORT METER application features

- Monitors and indicate fitness activities in real time with GPS

- Monitor the progress of the exercises

- Just run, cycling, jogging, walking, playing sports, drive a car and other activity with a one click

- Record personal statistics and stores them to compete with other athletes

- Save and stores your activities to cloud

- Statistics of the best athletes using application around the world

- Statistics for training activities (duration , distance , maximum speed , average speed) during training in running, biking, walking, driving, jogging, cycling, riding and etc.

- Improve your past activity results and see statistics immediately

- Social networks - Facebook login and Twitter login (Google+ login coming soon)

- See your end point of activity in Google Street View (intall for free in Play Store)

- Share fitness activities on Facebook and follow friends

- Challenges with other runners, bikers, drivers or other

- World map that show around the world activities.
